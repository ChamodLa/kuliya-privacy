Privacy Policy for Kuliya

Last Updated: 24/04/2026

AbeyLabs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Kuliya mobile application (the "App" or "Service") — a peer-to-peer rental marketplace operating in Sri Lanka. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Guest Access & Registration

You can browse listings, search for rental items, and view item details on Kuliya without creating an account. Account registration is only required for account-based actions, including:

  • Listing an item for rent
  • Sending rental requests or messages
  • Completing a rental transaction or payment
  • Submitting a review

This is in accordance with Apple App Store Guideline 5.1.1(v).

2. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Service, we may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

  • Name, Phone Number — for OTP-based account creation and identity display
  • Email Address — for account-related communications and support
  • WhatsApp Number — optionally provided, for rental party communication

b. Identity Verification (KYC)

To maintain a safe and trusted marketplace, verified users may be asked to provide:

  • National Identity Card (NIC) details and photos (Front/Back)
  • Residential address
  • A short verification video or selfie

KYC data is stored securely and used solely for identity verification within the platform.

c. Location Data

Why we use your location: Kuliya requests access to your device's precise and approximate location (Fine Location and Coarse Location) to display rental items available near you on the map — for example, showing all bikes, tools, or cameras listed within a 5 km radius of your current position — and to allow item owners to set an accurate pickup location for their listings. Location is only accessed while you are actively using the App. We do not track your location in the background.

d. Media & Files

With your explicit permission, we may access your device camera and photo library to:

  • Upload a profile photo (avatar)
  • Add item listing images
  • Document item condition before and after a rental (rental condition photos)

e. Device & Usage Data

  • Push notification token (to send rental updates and chat messages)
  • Device identifiers and IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
  • In-app activity logs and interactions (to improve the Service)
  • Crash reports and performance data (via Expo/Firebase)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, manage, and authenticate your account via phone OTP
  • To show you nearby rental listings based on your location
  • To facilitate rental transactions between owners and renters
  • To verify user identities (KYC) for a trusted marketplace
  • To process secure payments via third-party payment gateways
  • To send in-app chat messages, rental status updates, and system notifications
  • To moderate content, investigate reports, and enforce our community guidelines
  • To provide customer support and improve the App

4. Tracking & App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Kuliya does not use your personal data to track you across third-party apps and websites for advertising purposes, and we do not share your data with advertising networks or data brokers.

Some third-party SDKs integrated into the App (such as Firebase for crash analytics and performance monitoring) may collect aggregated, anonymised usage data. This data is used solely for improving the App's stability and performance and is not linked to your identity for advertising purposes.

If a future version of the App introduces any form of cross-app tracking, we will request your explicit permission through Apple's AppTrackingTransparency (ATT) framework before collecting any such data, in compliance with Apple App Store Guideline 5.1.2(i).

5. User-Generated Content & Community Standards

Kuliya is a user-generated content (UGC) platform. Users can post listings, upload photos, and communicate via the in-app chat. By using the App, you agree to our Terms of Use and End User Licence Agreement (EULA), which state that there is zero tolerance for objectionable, abusive, illegal, or harmful content.

Content Moderation

  • Automated filtering: Listing content is screened for prohibited keywords and categories before publication.
  • Community reporting: Any user can flag a listing or another user's profile as inappropriate using the in-app "Report" button available on every listing and profile page.
  • Blocking: Any user can block another user from the in-app "Block User" option on their profile. Blocking immediately removes the blocked user's content from your feed and notifies our moderation team.
  • 24-hour response commitment: Our team reviews all reported content within 24 hours. Content confirmed to be objectionable will be removed, and the offending user will be suspended or permanently banned from the platform.

What Constitutes Objectionable Content

  • Illegal items or prohibited goods
  • Hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory content
  • Misleading listings or fraudulent activity
  • Explicit, violent, or otherwise inappropriate imagery

By creating an account, you agree that AbeyLabs may remove any content and terminate any account that violates these standards, without prior notice.

6. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the Service:

  • Appwrite: Backend services, database, user authentication, and secure file/document storage.
  • Mapbox: Map rendering and location-based search queries.
  • Expo / Firebase: Push notification delivery, crash reporting, and app performance monitoring.
  • PayHere (or equivalent payment processors): Secure payment processing. We do not store card details on our servers.

Other users only see your public profile (Name, Avatar) and items you have listed. Phone numbers are only shared with confirmed rental parties to facilitate item handover.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES at rest) and secure cloud infrastructure (Appwrite) to protect your personal information and sensitive verification documents. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations.

Deleting Your Account: You can delete your account at any time from App Settings → Delete Account, or by emailing us at legal@abeylabs.dev. Upon verified request, we will permanently delete your profile, listings, and personal data, except where retention is required by applicable law (e.g., transaction records required for financial compliance).

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from minors. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy here and updating the "Last Updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

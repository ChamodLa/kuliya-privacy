Privacy Policy for Kuliya

Last Updated: 24/03/2026

AbeyLabs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Kuliya mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Service as a peer-to-peer rental marketplace in Sri Lanka, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, Phone Number (for OTP verification and communication), Email Address, and WhatsApp number.
  • Identity Verification Data (KYC): To ensure a trusted community, we collect Identity Card details, photos of your ID (Front/Back), residential address, and short verification videos.
  • Location Data: We request access to your device's location (Coarse and Fine Location) to show rental items near you on the map and to set accurate item locations.
  • Media & Files: We may access your device's camera and photo library (with your permission) to upload profile avatars, item images, and rental condition photos.
  • Device & Usage Data: Information such as your device push token (for notifications), IP address, app activity, and interactions within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, manage, and secure your account via phone OTP.
  • To facilitate rental transactions between owners and renters.
  • To verify user identities (KYC) to maintain a safe and trustworthy marketplace.
  • To display nearby rental items using location services.
  • To process payments (via third-party payment gateways like PayHere).
  • To send administrative notifications, chat messages, and updates.
  • To provide customer support and improve our application.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our Service:

  • Appwrite: Used for backend services, secure database management, user authentication, and secure file storage (images, KYC docs).
  • Mapbox: Used to render maps and process location-based queries.
  • Expo / Firebase: Used to deliver push notifications and monitor app performance.
  • PayHere (or other payment processors): Used for secure payment processing. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

Other users in the app will only see your public profile information (Name, Avatar) and items you have listed for rent. Phone numbers may be shared with confirmed rental parties to facilitate the handover.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud infrastructure (Appwrite) to protect your personal information and sensitive verification documents. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention & Account Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

User Data Deletion Request: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and personal data. You can delete your account directly from the App Settings or by contacting us. Upon request, we will delete your profile, listings, and associated data, except where retention is required by law (e.g., transaction records).

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 18 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
